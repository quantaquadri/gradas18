Our Love Story

There is only one happiness in this life, to love and be loved -- George Sand



The First Meet

I was posted to Rivers State for NYSC and was lucky to be posted to Porthacourt town for my PPA. I went to a tradefare with my friends Ada and Ify to do some shopping while in the process of shopping; Dasoye walked up to me with his younger brother behind him when he said "Hello"

First Impression

I turned around to see who was trying to talk to me when our eyes met I saw love in his eyes and acted calm like I don't known what he wanted from me. The question he asked me was; do you work in University of Science and Technology (UST) Rivers state? I said No.

Are your parents based in Porthacourt? I said No and he asked me what I was doing in Porthacourt. I just smiled cause I knew that's not the question he wanted to ask me, so he just collected my number and promise he will call me which he did the rest is now history.

The Proposal

He popped the question if I will marry him it was done in such a romantic way in a quiet and private place a place we 1st met and I said YES to his proposal. Am getting married to the love of my life. 28th July we will be walking down the aisle to say I Do.

Now We Getting Married

And we are getting married. For ever together Gracey and Dasoye
